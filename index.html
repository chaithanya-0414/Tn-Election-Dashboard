Poll Analyst's Corner: The TVK Factor

The 2026 election is defined by a multi-cornered contest unseen in recent history. While the DMK and AIADMK remain the primary contenders, the entry of Actor Vijay's **Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK)** has drastically altered vote calculations.

Pre-polls indicate TVK may only secure a handful of physical seats (1-5) due to the first-past-the-post system. However, their projected **11-13% vote share** makes them the ultimate "spoiler."

Key Scenarios:

  • Hung Assembly Risk: Increased probability if DMK drops below 40% vote share in tight constituencies.
  • Anti-Incumbency Split: TVK and AIADMK are currently dividing the anti-incumbency vote, inadvertently aiding the ruling DMK in triangulated fights.
  • Kongu Belt Battleground: Western TN remains the most fiercely contested region, with AIADMK showing strong retention.
